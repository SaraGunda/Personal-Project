Hello and Welcome to YogaMatch!

I'm Sara, and I created YogaMatch to make yoga more accessible to all. With YogaMatch, you can get a personalized flow for Free, and right to your inbox! Just complete the questionaire to be matched with the yoga flow for you.

Yoga is a connecting practice of mind, body, and spirit. Your practice is very specific to who you are, what is accessible for your body, and what you're looking to get out of your practice. This may change over time. The YogaMatch Quiz is designed to be taken again! Save your favorite flows. Please note, I am just a guide for these practices. Please set your own intention, and stay present. Don't hesitate to take any additional modifications your body needs. Enjoy your Flow!
